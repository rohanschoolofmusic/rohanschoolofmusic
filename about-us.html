About Us

RSM is an Online Music Institute that aims to redefine online music teaching and learning. We provide one-on-one sessions by professional faculties, with specialism in performance and musical theory. We also offer a range of courses in western classical music catering to different proficiency levels and preferences. The motivation behind the foundation of RSM was to address the challenges faced by the students undertaking online music sessions worldwide after personally mentoring them over years. Feedbacks provided by them was quintessential to improvise our digital infrastructure and teaching methodology. We concluded that no perfect course exist that can accommodate every student hence we drew inspiration from popular courses like Alfred’s, Leila Fletcher, John Thompson, etc. for the piano and Suzuki, Eta Cohen’s etc. for the violin and blended them together. We came up with an innovative and intuitive infrastructure that maximize the ease of learning and simulates an actual in-person session. 
We believe that every individual has their own objective behind endeavoring their musical journey, hence the sessions offered by us are entirely redesigned on case basis so that it’s aligned to each individual’s aspirations, age and learning pace. In addition, we prepare students for the graded music examination conducted by Trinity and ABRSM(Associated Board of Royal Schools of Music.)In an evolving and dynamic industry with lots of alternative online music academy and application platform offering music courses, how we stand out is by providing music education consultancy service to assess each student personally rather than putting them in a predefined framework, design their customized learning path so that we can meet their expectations in the least possible period and thus building a symbiotic bonding. Our objective is well defined as “It’s the journey that matters, not the destination” and we promise that the journey with us will yield a twin benefit of mastering the instrument as well as enrichment of your musical knowledge and passion.
